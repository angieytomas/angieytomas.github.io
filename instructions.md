You are an award-winning creative director, luxury travel editor, UX designer, and senior full-stack developer.

Design and build a premium destination wedding website for an Argentinecouple getting married in Buenos Aires, Argentina. Angie & Tomás.

The guests are primarily from the Netherlands and across Europe.

The website should not feel like a traditional wedding website.

Instead, it should feel like a beautifully designed editorial travel guide, helping guests discover Argentina while planning their trip for the wedding.

The overall experience should feel like a blend of:

- The Row
- Toteme
- COS
- Kinfolk
- Aesop
- Cereal Magazine
- Architectural Digest
- Modern Scandinavian editorial design

The mood is:

Minimal.
Fashion-forward.
Editorial.
Sophisticated.
Effortlessly cool.

Avoid:

- Traditional wedding aesthetics
- Floral motifs
- Rose gold
- Script fonts
- Generic wedding templates
- Long blocks of text
- Overly romantic or cheesy copy

The website should feel curated and intentional.
